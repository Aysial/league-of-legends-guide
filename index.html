Guide for Beginners

Improve Your Skills

Know Your Champions

One of the most important aspects of improving as a player is determined by how well you know your champions. It is recommended that you learn at least 3 champions per role.

Know Your Roles

Role mastery is key in improving your gameplay as this will seperate you between a good or bad player.

Communication

League of Legends is a team based game therefore it is incredibly important that you communicate well with your teammates to stay on top of your opponents.

Decision Making

Being able to distinguish between a bad or a good play will play a vital role in the long run and will ultimately make you a better player overall.
