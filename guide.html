League of Legends

Guide for Beginners

About

League of Legends is a team based 5v5 game. The goal is to destroy your opponents structures and ultimately defeat the opposing team. Players will control characters called champions who each have different movesets. Each champion is unique and there are over 150 to choose from.

Gameplay

Each champion can be classified into one of five roles. They are top laner, jungler, mid laner, attack damage carry and support. Knowing your preferred role in the game is key to get the most enjoyment out of this game.
